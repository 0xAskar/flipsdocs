Flips Discord Bot

Overview​

The Flips Discord Bot is one of the most comprehensive efficient NFT/Crypto Bot in the space. Currently, it is free. To add it your server, please visit Flips Discord page on our site.

We have a few batches of commands. Some are NFT specific, and the other half are Crypto specific. Also, you can visit the site to visualize and see all our commands.

Here is a list of all the commands, and how to use them:

NFT Commands​

StatisticDefinitionCommand
CfloorDisplays a collection's floor chart for a specific time frame./cfloor [symbols] [days]
CholdersDisplays a collection's holders chart for a specific time frame/cholders [symbols] [days]
ClistingsDisplays a collection's listings chart for a specific time frame/clistings [symbols] [days]
GasDisplays current average Ethereum gas price in gwei/gas
FloorDisplays a collection's current floor price and 24h change/floor
FloorDisplays a collection's current floor price and 24h change/floor [symbol]
HoldersDisplays a collection's current holder count and 24h change/holder [symbol]
RanksReturns the top 10 collection in order of 24h floor % change./ranks
StatsDisplays the past [day] stats for a collection./stats [symbol] [days]

Crypto Commands​

StatisticDefinitionCommand
ATHDisplays the all time high of a token./ath [token]
ATLDisplays the all time low of a token./atl [token]
CoinDisplays a token's price, daily change, and marketcap./coin [token]
Coin-ChartCharts the price of a token in USD over X number of days./coin-chart [token] [num_days]
Coin-Chart2Charts one token against another over X number of days./coin-chart2 [token1] [token2] [num_days]
Coin-ImageDisplay the current image of a token./coin-image [token]
RangeDisplays the all time high, low, and current price of a token./range [token]
TVLDisplays the total value locked of a protocol./tvl [token]
TVL-RatioCalculates the ratio of the total value locked over the market cap of a protocol./tvl-ratio [token]
